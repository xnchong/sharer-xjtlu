Welcome to Sharer@XJTLU.

This website aims to exchange information and build the bridge between students who has special expertise and students who wants to learn skills with lower cost within the campus.

This is not a commercial website and only provide a place for people to share things and help each other. It is highly recommended that anyone who is going to post either expertise or demand shall make the description as explicit and detailed as possible.
